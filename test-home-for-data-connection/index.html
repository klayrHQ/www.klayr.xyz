Staking

Ready to see your assets grow? Stake your tokens on a validator and start earning part of the rewards. Our high-yield staking rewards make it easy to earn passive income and maximize your returns while helping to secure the network. The longer you stake, the greater your rewards. Its time to put your assets to work and join the ranks of successful Klayniacs.

 

Dynamic Block Rewards

Our token, primarily a utility token, plays a crucial role in securing the blockchain. The average block reward is set to 2KLY, but the actual rewards are dynamically adjusted based on the validator weight. This ensures that both validating and staking are effectively incentivized. This dynamic approach encourages greater participation in network validation, leading to a more secure and robust system for all users. With a block time of only 7 seconds, rewards add up very fast!

WHAT IS KLAYR?

A BLOCKCHAIN APPLICATION PLATFORM

 

Klayr is designed to empower creators of all backgrounds and ambitions to build blockchain solutions. Whether youre an individual artist experimenting with your own tokens or NFT collections or a developer creating full-fledged blockchain applications on your dedicated sidechain, the platform provides the tools you need.

 

Sidechain Interoperability

Flexibility for developers to scale and tailor their blockchain projects to their specific needs.

Mainchain Token Issuance

Klayr simplifies token issuance and NFT collection creation, offering accessibility to all through unparalleled ease of use.

EVM bridge

A gateway to set up wrapped tokens on the Ethereum network, thereby expanding reach and accessibility to a broader audience and deeper liquidity pools.

Start Building

Building on the blockchain has never been this straightforward. With our easy-to-use SDK, you can dive into blockchain development using just Javascript. No steep learning curve, no complicated jargonjust pure innovation. Get your ideas off the ground faster with Klayr.
